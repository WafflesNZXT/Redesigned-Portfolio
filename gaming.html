Gaming

Gaming is the type of thing that I really want, I don't really need, but is very fun. I'm especially good at Rocket League, a competitive game that mixes soccer with cars. Besides playing games to win, I like being creative in games like Minecraft and Roblox, building and exploring different worlds, modes, and genres. I also consider myself a car guy, so I enjoy playing realistic driving games like Forza Horizon 5 and American Truck Simulator, which become even more realistic with my Logitech G27 + Shifter & Pedals. I also enjoy playing fast and short games like Super Animal Royale, which is basically animals fighting each other in a closed map with weapons. Along with just playing games, I also stream and make videos of my gameplay, you can check my YouTube channel here.
